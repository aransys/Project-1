What Worcester says about us

Good questions

Frequently asked

How quickly do you turn orders around?

Standard turnaround is 24 hours from pickup to delivery. Same-day service is available before 10am for an extra £6.

Is delivery really free?

Yes — pickup and delivery are free for any order over £20 within Worcester. Smaller orders carry a £4 delivery fee.

What detergents do you use?

Plant-based, dermatologically-tested detergents by default. We carry hypoallergenic and fragrance-free options at no extra cost — just let us know.

What if an item is damaged?

Every order is inspected pre- and post-clean. In the unlikely event of damage, we offer up to 10× the cleaning charge per item, no quibbles.

Do you handle bedding and curtains?

Absolutely — duvets, sheets, curtains, and large rugs are all part of our regular service. Get a custom quote on the contact form below.
